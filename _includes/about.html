About Us

At Hachi & Co—fondly known as Hachico—we believe in creating possibilities. Inspired by the octopus, our name reflects its eight tentacles, a symbol of versatility, adaptability, and expertise. In Japanese, “Hachi” means “eight,” while “Hachiko” holds a special place in our hearts as the middle name of our feisty and ever-curious dog. Like Hachiko, who embraces the world with boundless energy and a thirst for discovery, we approach every challenge with a passion for learning, adapting, and crafting innovative solutions.

Hachico is more than just a company; it’s a philosophy. We look at the big picture without losing sight of the details. By simplifying complexity, amplifying ideas, and collaborating with care, we deliver solutions that resonate and results that matter.
